Learning Proverbs

Inspirational Proverbs

  1. 三分天注定，七分靠打拼。 sann-hun thinn tsù-tiānn, tshit-hun khò phah-piànn.
    Only thirty percent is destined by fate, another seventy percent is depend on hard working.
  2. 一枝草，一點露。 Tsi̍t ki tsháu, tsi̍t tiám lōo.
    As long as one is still breathing, one has a chance to live. There is always a way out.
  3. 三百六十行，行行出狀元。 Sann-pah la̍k-tsa̍p hâng, hâng-hâng tshut tsiōng-guân.
    In every trade, a master appears. You can produce outstanding achievements in any task, provided you put it enough love and diligence.
  4. 愛拚才會贏。 Ài piànn tsiah ē iânn.
    You'll always have to fight before you can win.

Describing characteristics

  1. 人心肝，牛腹肚。 Lâng sim-kuann, gû pak-tóo.
    Human’s heart, but with cow’s belly. Describes that a person is greedy.
  2. 目睭看懸，無看低。 Ba̍k-tsiu khuànn-kuân, bô khuànn-kē.
    Describes that when walking or doing things people only care about the illusory distance or the rich and powerful, instead of looking at the things or people in front of them.
  3. 見笑轉受氣。 Kiàn-siàu tńg siū-khì.
    become angry from shame
  4. 恬恬食三碗公半。 Tiām-tiām tsia̍h sann uánn-kong puànn.
    A metaphor for a person silently doing something surprising or admirable

Philosophy for life

  1. 一分錢，一分貨。 Tsi̍t hun tsînn, tsi̍t hun huè.
    You get what you pay for.
  2. 一日討海，三日曝網。 Tsi̍t ji̍t thó-hái, sann ji̍t pha̍k bāng.
    to work by fits and starts
  3. 一樣米飼百樣人。 Tsi̍t iūnn bí tshī pah-iūnn lâng.
    Everybody thinks and acts a little differently even when we are in the same environment.
  4. 人情留一線，日後好相看。 Jîn-tsîng lâu tsi̍t suànn, ji̍t-āu hó sio-khuànn.
    Leave room for everything and we can get along with other easily. A metaphor for being kind to others.
