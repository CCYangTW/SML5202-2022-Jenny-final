Vocabulary

toh-pòo 桌布
Meaning rag
Example Sentence 你愛共這塊桌布小沐一下仔水，才有法度拭桌頂。Lí ài kā tsit tè toh-pòo sió bak tsi̍t-ē-á tsuí, tsiah ū huat-tōo tshit toh-tíng.
Thôo-kha 塗跤
Meaning floor
Example Sentence 你的物件落佇塗跤矣。Lí ê mi̍h-kiānn lak tī thôo-kha--ah.
Tshit-á 拭仔
Meaning eraser
Example Sentence 你的拭仔敢會當借我？Lí ê tshit-á kám ē-tàng tsioh--guá?
Kim-sun 金孫
Meaning grandchild
Example Sentence 阿英有真濟金孫。 A-ing ū tsin tsē kim-sun.
Tshuì-khí 喙齒
Meaning tooth
Example Sentence 鬥喙齒　tàu tshuì-khí
phòng-í 膨椅
Meaning coach
Example Sentence 一組膨椅，會當坐十外冬，毋通凊彩。tsi̍t-tsoo phòng-í, ē-tàng tsē tsa̍p-guā tang, m̄-thang tshìn-tshái.
Sap-bûn 雪文
Meaning soap
Example Sentence 若欲預防病毒，洗手著用雪文會加較有效。Nā beh ī-hông pēnn-to̍k, sé tshiú tio̍h iōng sap-bûn ē ke khah ū hāu.
Bîn-tshn̂g 眠床
Meaning bed
Example Sentence 囡仔大漢了後，眠床就愛換新的矣。Gín-á tuā-hàn liáu-āu, bîn-tshn̂g tō ài uānn sin--ê--ah.
tiān-hong 電風
Meaning electric fan
Example Sentence 熱天無法度無電風。Jua̍h-thinn bô-huat-tōo bô tiān-hong.
Ta̍k-ke 逐家
Meaning everyone
Example Sentence 逐家攏是家己人，著愛互相照顧。Ta̍k-ke lóng sī ka-kī-lâng, tio̍h-ài hōo-siong tsiàu-kòo.
Thih-bé 鐵馬
Meaning bicycle
Example Sentence 騎鐵馬四界走。 Khiâ thih-bé sì-kè tsáu.
Gue̍h-niû 月娘
Meaning Moon
Example Sentence 下昏暗月娘真光。Ē-hng-àm gue̍h-niû tsin kng.
Sì-kè 四界
Meaning everywhere
Example Sentence 伊真𠢕四界走。 I tsin gâu sì-kè tsáu.
Tsáu 走
Meaning run
Example Sentence 伊走無去矣。I tsáu bô--khì--ah.
ūn-tōng-tiânn 運動埕
Meaning sports field
Example Sentence 明仔早起愛佇運動埕集合。 Bîn-á-tsá-khí ài tī ūn-tōng-tiânn tsi̍p-ha̍p.
tik-tshân-enn/inn 竹田嬰
Meaning hopter
Example Sentence 以早的囡仔攏會曉做竹田嬰耍。Í-tsá ê gín-á lóng ē-hiáu tsò tik-tshân-enn sńg.
kan-lo̍k 干樂
Meaning whipping top
Example Sentence 囡仔當咧拍干樂。Gín-á tng teh phah kan-lo̍k.
tshit-thô-mi̍h 𨑨迌物
Meaning toy
Example Sentence 囡仔人愛耍𨑨迌物仔。Gín-á-lâng ài sńg tshit-thô-mih-á.
tó-uī 佗位
Meaning where
Example Sentence 恁兜是蹛佇佗位？Lín tau sī tuà tī tó-uī?
înn-á 圓仔
Meaning tangyuan
Example Sentence 我想欲食圓仔。Guá siūnn-beh tsia̍h înn-á.
bô-îng 無閒
Meaning I am not available
Example Sentence 伊的工課真濟，足無閒的。I ê khang-khuè tsin tsē, tsiok bô-îng--ê.
tshing-khì 清氣
Meaning clean
Example Sentence 我的房間不時攏真清氣。Guá ê pâng-king put-sî lóng tsin tshing-khì.
tsia̍h tsá-tǹg 食早頓
Meaning eat breakfast
Example Sentence 你有食早頓無？Lí ū tsia̍h tsá-tǹg--bô?
hó-ka-tsài 好佳哉
Meaning fortunately
Example Sentence 好佳哉有你佇咧，無我就慘矣。Hó-ka-tsài ū lí tī--leh, bô guá tō tshám--ah.
suí-suí 媠媠
Meaning beautiful
Example Sentence 伊逐工攏穿甲媠媠。 I ta̍k-kang lóng tshīng kah suí-suí.
sî-kiânn 時行
Meaning fashionable
Example Sentence 伊的穿插真時行。I ê tshīng-tshah tsin sî-kiânn.
pōng thé-un 磅體溫
Meaning take/check one's temperature
Example Sentence 你去提度針來磅體溫。 Lí khì the̍h tōo-tsiam lâi pōng thé-un.
káu-hiā 狗蟻
Meaning ant
Example Sentence 雞卵糕愛囥冰箱，才袂生狗蟻。 Ke-nn̄g-ko ài khǹg ping-siunn, tsiah bē senn káu-hiā.
Tsia̍h-pá--buē 食飽未
Meaning How are you?
Literal Meaning: Are you full yet?
Example Sentence 你食飽未？Lí tsia̍h-pá--buē?
Onn-onn-khùn 唔唔睏
Meaning Sleeping (informal)
Example Sentence 嬰仔唔唔睏，一暝大一寸。Enn-á onn-onn-khùn, tsi̍t mê tuā tsi̍t tshùn.
